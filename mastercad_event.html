About The Event

Performance, reliability and market are what drives the designs in modern engineering. You might find a lot of designs for the same thing but not all of them make it to the top. Do you have what it takes to make a perfect engineering design? ​MasterCAD 2.0 ​is a unique one of its kind competition where designing, analysis and simulation skills along with innovation, reasoning, & logic are put to the test.


Guidelines

Each team can have a maximum of 3 members. It will be a 5 hr hackathon where a problem statement will be given and the best optimal design needs to designed and modelled.
