About The Event

Made an innovative project that will help people in some day-to-day problems or have a business idea that will move investors? Dinnovent is just the platform you needed. Showcase your Technical and Entrepreneurial Intellect to compete with several others to Win over the judges’ hearts.


Guidelines

There are two categories in the competition

Category A : Technical Project/Idea Presentation:

In this category, the team will showcase its Technical projects or Ideas to the Judges. Novelty and Impact of ideas will be judged.

Category B : Business Plan Presentation:

In this category, the team will showcase their Start-up or Business Ideas to the Judges. Novelty and Impact of ideas will be judged.
