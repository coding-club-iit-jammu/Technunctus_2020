About The Event

You can never improve without good competition and that’s why we bring you the Technunctus Programming contest. Test your skills against your peers and prove your Worth. Solve some innovative problems through coding and prove your programming and Algorithmic skills to test.


Guidelines

The competition will have two rounds

Round 1 will be an online round in which contestants can participate in groups of size 2 and the top 20 teams will be selected for the onsite round. It would be conducted on HackerEarth. The round would have 5-6 questions which have to be solved in a duration of 2 hrs.

Round 2 would be an onsite round and the top 3 teams will be given prizes and goodies.
