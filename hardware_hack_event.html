About The Event

Designing and Simulation might feel easy when it comes to actually make the product. It takes a lot of patience and brainstorming while actually making the hardware. Hardware Hackathon is just what you need to put your hardware development skills to test. This event will have two sections: Workshop and Hackathon.


Guidelines

The Event has two sections

1. Workshop- On day 1, participants will be given the opportunity to learn a basic platform and have hands-on experience

2. Hackathon- On day 2, participants will be provided with a problem statement, basic electronic components, tools and any other hardware that they may need. Within 8 hours, teams will have to come up with a solution to the given problem statement.

It is a team event. No. of participants per team to be updated later.

Individual registration for the workshop/hackathon is also possible.

Problem Statement will be declared on the spot.
